Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Color PPF and vinyl wraps?

Color PPF offers superior protection against rock chips and scratches while providing a premium finish. Unlike vinyl wraps, it features self-healing technology and maintains a factory-like appearance.

How long does installation take?

Installation typically takes 2-3 days for a full vehicle, depending on the complexity of the vehicle and specific coverage areas.

Does Color PPF protect against scratches?

Yes, EVOLV Color PPF provides excellent protection against scratches, rock chips, and UV damage. Minor scratches even disappear with heat thanks to our self-healing technology.

How do I maintain my Color PPF finish?

Simple wash and care with pH-neutral car soap is all that's needed. The hydrophobic coating helps repel dirt and contaminants, making maintenance easy.

What's the warranty?

EVOLV Color PPF comes with a 10-year warranty against yellowing, cracking, and peeling when installed by a certified installer.
